Oklahoma is one of the most storm-intensive states in the nation. Tulsa sits in Green Country, where severe thunderstorms, tornadoes, baseball-sized hail, and multi-inch rainfall events occur every spring. When severe weather damages your roof or forces water through your walls, the secondary water damage can be as destructive as the storm itself.

Common Storm Damage Water Issues in Tulsa

  • Roof damage allowing water intrusion into attic and living spaces
  • Window frame failure and wind-driven rain penetration
  • Flash flooding from overwhelmed storm drains

  • Hail damage to roof leading to subsequent water leaks
  • Tornado damage with complete structural water exposure
