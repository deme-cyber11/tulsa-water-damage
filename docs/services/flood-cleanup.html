Flood vs. Water Damage Insurance Coverage

There's an important distinction Tulsa homeowners need to understand: standard homeowner's insurance covers sudden water intrusion from storms (wind-driven rain, roof damage, etc.) but does NOT cover rising floodwater from ground level. For that, you need a separate National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP) policy. We can help you determine which policy applies and work with your adjuster directly.
