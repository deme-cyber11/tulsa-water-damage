What to Do While You Wait

  • Turn off the water supply if you can locate the shutoff valve
  • Turn off electricity in affected areas (only if safe to do so)
  • Move valuables and electronics to dry areas
  • Do NOT use a regular vacuum to remove water
  • Document the damage with photos for your insurance claim
